A Few Things I Never Want to Forget

Hi, babiii. 🤍

Before you start looking around this little website, I just wanted to tell you something.

I started making this because I wanted to give you something that would last longer than flowers or a simple message. I wanted to make something you could always come back to whenever you missed me, whenever you wanted to smile, or whenever you just needed a little reminder of how much you mean to me.

While I was making this, I kept catching myself smiling at my screen. Every page reminded me of a memory, a conversation, or one of those random little moments that probably seemed ordinary, but somehow became my favorite parts of us.

I spent more time than I'd like to admit choosing colors, changing words, deleting sentences, and rewriting them over and over because I wanted everything to feel right. I wanted every page to feel warm, every little detail to feel special, and most of all, I wanted this website to feel like you.

There were moments when I just stared at a blank screen because I couldn't figure out how to put my feelings into words. It's funny how I can talk to you for hours, but when it comes to writing about how much I love you, suddenly every word feels too small. No matter how much I wrote, it never felt like enough to describe what you mean to me.

I hope that as you scroll through these pages, you don't just see a website. I hope you see the time, effort, and love that went into every little detail. Maybe it isn't perfect, and maybe there are things I could've done better, but every single part of this was made with you on my mind.

Everything you're about to see is a little piece of my heart. Some pages are sweet, some are a little silly, and some might even make you laugh at how cheesy I am. But every single one exists because I wanted you to know just how deeply you're loved.

So... take your time. There's no need to rush. Explore every page, every little surprise, and every memory I tucked into this website. I hope it brings a smile to your face the same way making it brought one to mine.

I made all of this just for you. 🤍

Always yours
